Mission & Products

What we sell and who we sell it to

Our Mission

Make AI education accessible to every Indian — in Hindi and English — so they can learn, earn, and grow in the digital economy.

Target audience: Students, job seekers, freelancers, and small business owners in India. Tier 1, 2 and 3 cities. Ages 18–35.

Core Principle

Free Value First, Paid Product Later. Become the most helpful AI resource in India for free. Convert that trust into sales.

Never sell without first giving value. Every piece of content points back to a product.

Sales Strategy

6-phase growth plan to build, launch and scale

Phase 1 — Week 1–2

Build the Machine

Free Mini-Ebook (Growth Asset #1)

Create a free 10-page PDF: "7 ChatGPT Prompts That Changed My Life"

  • Uses existing prompt content from paid ebook
  • LearnerMachine branding on every page
  • Last page = full-page ad + QR code to paid ebook
  • People share freely — it markets itself

Distribute everywhere: Instagram bio, WhatsApp, Telegram, LinkedIn, Twitter, Quora, Reddit

WhatsApp Community Setup

Create "AI Seekho India — LearnerMachine" using WhatsApp Communities (newer feature, underused)

Sub-groups:

  • #daily-ai-tip — one tip every morning
  • #prompt-of-the-day — one prompt daily
  • #earnings-proof — members share wins
  • #questions — live Q&A

Why: WhatsApp has 95%+ open rates in India vs 20% email.

Phase 2 — Ongoing

Viral Content Engine

Faceless Reels Formula (Instagram + YouTube Shorts)

One short video every day. 30-second structure:

Reel Structure
0–3 sec:   HOOK — Shocking or relatable opening line
4–20 sec:  VALUE — Show ChatGPT screen, demonstrate the tip
21–27 sec: PROOF — One result, one number, one screenshot
28–30 sec: CTA — "Follow for daily AI tips" or "Link in bio"

Post to Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, LinkedIn Video — same video, three platforms.

Tools: CapCut (edit) + ElevenLabs (AI voice) + Canva (thumbnail) + ChatGPT (script)

LinkedIn Newsletter — Untapped Goldmine

Name: "AI for India — Weekly Digest"

Post weekly stories like: "How a 22-year-old from Jaipur earned ₹40,000 this month using ChatGPT"

Why LinkedIn works: Newsletters notify ALL connections automatically. B2B angle = companies, coaching institutes, HR discover you. Growing fast in Tier 2/3 India.

End every newsletter with: "This week's prompt pack is from our AI for Beginners ebook — ₹499 at learnermachine.com"

Phase 3 — Month 1

Referral Machine

Pay ₹499, Get ₹100 Back Per Friend

  1. Customer buys ebook for ₹499
  2. They get a unique referral code
  3. For every friend who buys → they get ₹100 UPI cashback
  4. Their friend also gets ₹50 off
Math: 100 buyers × 2 referrals each = 200 new customers with zero ad spend. Put referral offer on last page of ebook itself.

Free for Educators Campaign

Reach out to college professors, YouTube educators (1K–50K subs), coaching institute owners.

Offer: Free copy + 30% affiliate commission on every student they refer.

One professor with 200 students = 20–40 immediate sales.

Setup: Use Instamojo's built-in affiliate system or Google Form + manual tracking to start.

Phase 4 — Month 2

Community-Led Viral Growth

#30DaysAIChallenge — Highest Viral Potential

Launch a 30-day challenge. Each day = one small AI task. People post progress on Instagram/LinkedIn with #30DaysAIChallenge and tag LearnerMachine.

Prize: LearnerMachine completion certificate (Canva, 10 min to make) — people love sharing certificates on LinkedIn.

Why it goes viral: User-generated content = free marketing. Social proof. People who complete become buyers of advanced products.

Phase 5 — Month 2 (When Budget Ready)

Paid Ads — The Right Way

Meta Ads Strategy — Don't Sell Directly

Never run ads to sell the ₹499 ebook directly. Instead:

Ad Funnel
Ad: "Free Download — 7 ChatGPT Prompts for Indians"
↓
Landing page: Collect email, deliver free PDF
↓
Email sequence: 5 emails over 7 days
↓
Email 5: Sell the paid ebook at ₹499

Budget: Start ₹200/day → 15–40 email subscribers daily at ₹5–15/lead

Targeting: Age 18–35, India, Interests: ChatGPT / Digital Marketing / Freelancing / Make Money Online / Startup India

Micro-Influencer Strategy

Target accounts with 5K–50K followers in: freelancing, student life, digital marketing, career advice India niches.

Offer: Free ebook + 40% commission on every sale.

Why micro: 8–15% engagement vs mega's 0.5–2%. More authentic. Usually free for ebook + commission.

DM Template
"Hey [name], love your content. We made an AI ebook for Indians
(Hindi + English) that your audience would love. Would you
be open to a free copy + earn ₹200 per sale you drive?
No pressure at all."

Engagement Rules

  • 30-minute rule: Reply to every comment in the first 30 mins after posting. Algorithm rewards fast engagement.
  • DM strategy: When someone comments "interested" — DM them the link personally. 40–60% conversion rate.
  • 45-min/day workflow: 30 min morning (caption + design + edit) + 15 min evening (stories + replies + DMs)
  • Batch content: Every Sunday, create all 7 posts for the week. Schedule free with Meta Business Suite.
