🎥 Face Reel — Monologue

"AI ne meri zindagi
badal di — seriously"

Relatable personal story. Hook with a real moment. Build trust through vulnerability.

⏰ Post at 9:00 AM
📹 Shoot Notes — Before You Film
  • Sit or stand — casual setting (desk, room, café). Doesn't need to be perfect.
  • Wear something clean and solid-colored. Orange or white works great with your brand.
  • Film in portrait mode (9:16). Natural light from a window — not overhead light.
  • Speak directly to camera like you're talking to one friend, not an audience.
  • No teleprompter — know the 4 beats and speak naturally. It should feel unscripted.
  • Total target length: 45–60 seconds. Film 2–3 takes, use the most natural one.
🎥 Full Monologue Script — Speak These Words Naturally
HOOK — 0 to 4 sec (say this confidently, pause after)

"Ek cheez hai jo maine 6 mahine pehle start ki thi — aur honestly, mujhe pata nahi tha ki ye itna kuch badal degi."

STORY — 4 to 25 sec

"Pehle main wo log hota tha jo har cheez ke liye Google karta tha. Assignment ho, resume ho, ya koi bhi sawaal ho — Google, scroll, scroll, scroll. Bahut time jaata tha aur result average rehta tha."

"Fir kisi ne bataya — ChatGPT try karo. Maine socha, ye bhi koi YouTube tutorial hoga jisme kuch kaam nahi aayega. But try kiya."

"Pehle hi din — ek prompt likhke maine ek complete project proposal generate ki. Jo kaam mujhe 3 ghante leta — 12 minute mein ho gaya."

TURN — 25 to 40 sec (leaning in moment)

"Aur tab samjha — problem tool ki nahi thi. Problem ye thi ki mujhe pata hi nahi tha sahi tarike se use kaise karte hain."

"Right prompt likhna — that's the actual skill. Aur jab ye aata hai, toh AI literally ek personal assistant ban jaata hai."

CTA — 40 to 50 sec

"Agar tum bhi abhi tak AI ko sirf experiment ki tarah use kar rahe ho — aaj follow karo. Main har roz ek practical AI tip share karta hoon. Koi theory nahi. Seedha kaam ki cheez." Camera pe smile. Natural stop.

Text Overlays (CapCut)

0–4s: "Maine 6 mahine pehle ek cheez start ki..."

12s: "3 ghante ka kaam = 12 minute" (bold, centered)

28s: "Sahi prompt = personal assistant"

42s: "Follow karo → Daily AI tips 🔥"

Production Details

Audio: Use a currently trending Hindi/lo-fi beat at 20% volume underneath your voice

Cuts: Jump cut every 4–6 seconds to keep energy up

End card: Freeze last frame + add text "Follow @learnermachine"

Thumbnail: Your face mid-expression with text "AI ne sab badal diya"

Caption

🎥 Face Reel — Monologue

"The AI Mistake That
Cost Me 3 Months"

Vulnerable, honest, educational. Talk about a real mistake. This is the highest trust-building content type — admitting failure.

⏰ Post at 10:00 AM
📹 Shoot Notes
  • Calm, reflective energy — not high energy. Like telling a story to a friend over chai.
  • Can film this sitting down, leaning forward slightly — creates intimacy.
  • No heavy editing — a few cuts is enough. Let the words breathe.
  • This reel intentionally has less text overlay. Let the face and voice do the work.
  • Slight pause after the "mistake reveal" at the 15 second mark. Let it land.
🎥 Full Monologue Script
HOOK — 0 to 5 sec

"Main AI use kar raha tha 3 mahine se — aur ek bhi kaam dhang se nahi ho raha tha. Aur galti meri thi."

THE MISTAKE — 5 to 22 sec

"Problem ye thi — main ChatGPT se pooch raha tha jaise Google se poochte hain. Short, vague questions. 'Give me marketing ideas.' 'Write me a post.' Aur fir answer average aata tha, main disappoint ho jaata tha."

"Sochta tha — AI overhyped hai. Kuch khas nahi. Ye bahut log bolte hain."

(pause — look directly at camera)

"But actually — main hi galat tha."

THE LESSON — 22 to 38 sec

"Jab samjha ki AI ek tool hai — aur tool se sahi kaam lene ke liye sahi instruction dena padta hai — sab badal gaya."

"Context do. Role do. Format batao. Specific raho. Ye 4 cheezein seekhi aur suddenly AI ne woh kaam dene shuru kiye jo actually kaam aaye."

THE GIVE — 38 to 50 sec

"Ye galti mat karna. Agar AI se average results aa rahe hain — prompt better karo, tool nahi badlo."

"Aur agar sahi prompt likhna seekhna hai — bio mein ek complete guide hai. Free bhi hai, paid bhi. Aap choose karo." Smile. Done.

Text Overlays

0–5s: "3 mahine. AI. Zero results. Kyun? 🤔"

18s: "Main galat tha — AI nahi 😅"

28s: "Context + Role + Format + Specificity = 🔥"

44s: "Bio mein hai — free guide + full ebook"

Why This Performs

Admitting you were wrong is the most relatable thing a creator can do. Your audience is currently making the same mistake — they'll recognize themselves in your story.

"Main galat tha" is a pattern interrupt. No one expects that in a creator reel. It stops the scroll.

Caption

3 mahine mujhe laga — AI mujhse kuch zyada smart nahi hai. 😂

Fir realize kiya: main hi galat pooch raha tha.

Prompt likhna ek skill hai. Aur ye skill seekhne mein literally 1 din lagta hai.

Sabse badi AI tip jo kisi ne mujhe di: "Jitna zyada context doge — utna better result milega."

Ye mistake mat karo. Seedha sahi se shuru karo. 🙏
